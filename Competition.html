Final Round

The Sky Observatory Competition will be held under 6 factions:

  • 1) Moon Mapping
  • 2) Deep Sky Object Observations
  • 3) Constellation Mapping
  • 4) Planetary Observations
  • 5) Theory Paper
  • 6) Spot Test and Viva Test

The team accumulating the highest number of scores will be crowned as the Champions of Star Party 2026.
