The Astronomical Workshop

The Star Party '26 Astronomical workshop will be held with the prospect of introducing astronomy to the newcomers from schools, islandwide. This session serves as a gateway for young minds to explore the mysteries of the cosmos.

Discussion Focus

This workshop will mainly focus on discussions under:

  • General Astronomy
  • Observational Astronomy
  • Rocketry

Conducted By

The workshop will be conducted by the Old Anandians' Astronomical Association, Old Mayans Astronomical Association, and the Star Party Crew.

Valuable tutor guides and handbooks will be distributed to all the participants on this venture.

Award: "Uprising Team of Star Party 2026"

At the conclusion of the workshop, participants will be tested with a theory paper and a practical test. The team which scores the highest will be awarded the prestigious title of the Uprising Team of Star Party 2026.
