Astrophotography Competition

Envisaged in 2014 to launch concurrently with Star Party, this competition has set a benchmark for astronomical quiz and photography events sub-continent wide.The competition is conducted in two distinct stages:

Stage 01: Preliminary Round

Competitors must submit astronomy-related photographs based on the official event theme. These submissions will be evaluated by a panel of professionals.


- Lunar and Planetary Photography
- Deep Sky Object Photography
- Night Sky Photography

Stage 02: Final Round

Successful competitors from the preliminary round will advance to an on-site competition held at the University of Peradeniya.


The Astrophotography Round requires participants to capture photographs of celestial objects under the given categories using the their own equipment. Participants must then present and explain their photographs to the board of judges, providing details about the subject, method of capture, and relevant observational aspects within a given time frame.

People's Choice Award

The top 5 photographs from each category in the preliminary round will enter a voting procedure across all Star Party social media platforms. The photograph with the most votes will be crowned the Most Popular Astro Photograph of its category.
