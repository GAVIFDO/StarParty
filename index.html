Anandian Astronomical Association

Founded in 1998, the Anandian Astronomical Association has been a pioneer in inspiring young minds to explore the universe. For over two decades, it has promoted astronomy among students at Ananda College and across Sri Lanka through initiatives that blend education with celestial wonder.

Astronomical Society Of Mahamaya Girls’ College Kandy

The Astronomical Society of Mahamaya Girls' College, Kandy marks a rich legacy that began in 1996, celebrating a journey filled with excellence and triumph. Over the years, the society has woven an illustrious path, reaching remarkable milestones and fostering a spirit of exploration and dedication in the field of astronomy..
