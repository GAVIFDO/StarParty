Online Sub-competitions

Parallel to the main event, a recently introduced series of online sub-competitions is held to showcase the diverse talents of our astronomical community. These events allow participants to express their creativity and analytical skills from anywhere in the island.

The Categories

The sub-competitions mainly consist of:

  • Science Fiction Writing: Crafting stories that blend scientific theory with imaginative futures.
  • Manual Arts: Physical artwork, sketches, and models inspired by the cosmos.
  • Digital Arts: Digital paintings, 3D renders, and graphic designs of celestial themes.
  • Decoding Competition: A test of logic and cryptographical skill to uncover hidden astronomical messages.

Recognition & Awards

The top three competitors of each category will be rewarded with a valuable certificate at the main Star Party event held at the University of Peradeniya.
